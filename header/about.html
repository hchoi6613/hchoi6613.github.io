Fun facts about me

Hi, I'm Hana! I was born in Korea and moved to Chicago suburbs with my family when I was 11.

My passion for design started when I took my first Photoshop class in high school. I immediately fell in love with how easily it was to change around things in computer and craved for learning more about different design programs since then.

My previous experience includes internships and part-time roles at Link Logistics, along with freelance projects for food and beverage businesses. My interest in UX design began when I discovered the power of research-driven solutions, understanding that effective design extends beyond visual aesthetics.

I'm currently working at SO Dsgn, a Chicago-based brand and industrial design studio. My role involves developing brand and UI/UX solutions for a diverse clients.
